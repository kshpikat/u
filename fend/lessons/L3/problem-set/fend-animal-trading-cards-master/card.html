Tiger

Tigers are Mighty But Vulnerable...

  • Population: Around 3,890
  • Scientific Name: Panthera tigris
  • Weight: 220-660 pounds
  • Length: 4-10 feet

The largest of all the Asian big cats, tigers rely primarily on sight and sound rather than smell. They typically hunt alone and stalk prey. A tiger can consume up to 88 pounds of meat at one time.
